Essential Travel Tips

Pack light and bring versatile clothing.
Keep digital and physical copies of important documents.
Learn basic phrases of the local language.
Use travel apps for navigation and booking.
Stay hydrated and take care of your health.
Always have travel insurance for emergencies.
Notify your bank before traveling to avoid card issues.
Research local customs and etiquette to respect the culture.
Use public transport to save money and explore like a local.
Keep a small first-aid kit for minor injuries or illnesses.
